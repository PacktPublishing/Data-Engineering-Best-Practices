"Elsevier launches EmBiology to deliver the unparalleled insights into biological activities that accelerate drug discovery"; by Elsevier, April 19, 2023;

EmBiology (https://www.elsevier.com/products/embiology) provides access to biological relationships and pathways. It can visualize millions of biological relationships to understand disease biology. It can expose targeted data using filters for: publications, relations, and concepts. The product reduces the risk of research bias by removing the reliance on known search terms.
