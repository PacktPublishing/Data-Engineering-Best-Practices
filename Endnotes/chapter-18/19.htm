"EmBiology Provides Fast Access to Biological Relationships and Pathways"; by Elsevier; 2024;

EmBiology enables you to visualize millions of biological relationships to understand disease biology. It enables you also to acquire data using publication, relation, and concept filters. This helps minimize research risks and bias by removing the use of sole source publications and common researcher favored search terms. In introduction to the Biology Knowledge Graph, one can see the dataset that powers EmBiology (https://videos.ctfassets.net/o78em1y1w4i4/2A2auTkQeMsGbPvpRmgpzI/21d173ad78701449fefb3f3126f51637/Biology-Knowledge-Graph_Elsevier_Original.mp4).

The data is searchable or can be licensed off-platform for analytics and modeling purposes. See the EmBiology Fact Sheet: (https://assets.ctfassets.net/o78em1y1w4i4/1o4TSxumH9ZFQFc2cd27Qf/16dd4d6afb7621db4d5655478f00d3e5/EmBiology_Fact_Sheet.pdf).
