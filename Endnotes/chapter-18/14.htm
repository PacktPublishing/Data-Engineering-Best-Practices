Maulik Kkamdar; presenter at The Knowledge Graph Conference; 2021;

Maulik Kamdar, PhD in Biomedical Informatics from Stanford University currently is the Director of Clinical Informatics at Optum Health.

Research interests are:

1.      Semantic Web,

2.      information retrieval,

3.      ML / NLP,

4.      graph analytics,

5.      visualization,

6.      biomedical informatics, and

7.      operationalized data science.

He has worked to develop novel data science methods, enterprise knowledge graphs, and features within Web-based applications that have 10K+ users, such as:

1.      KPOrg Digital Platform,

2.      Elsevier's ClinicalKey Search,

3.      WebProtege,

4.      Data.Gov.IE and

5.      the Reactome.Org Pathway Browser.

His research was awarded the American Medical Informatics Association (AMIA) 2020 Dissertation Award.

Refer to https://www.knowledgegraph.tech/speakers/maulik-kamdar.
