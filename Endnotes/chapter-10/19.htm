"TestOps: The Future of Software Testing"; What is 'TestOps?'

'TestOps' is the process of automating and centralizing streamlined planning, monitoring, and testing of software.

Disjointed, siloed teams and processes are integrated into a test engine to create quality software, faster, and with fewer defects.

'DevOps' ensures IT operations necessary to run the SDLC; and 'TestOps,' ensures processes and IT operations for the test framework to make sure that software gets delivered optimally without negatively impacting quality metrics.
