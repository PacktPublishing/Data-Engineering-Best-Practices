"Why Agile Fails in Large Enterprises"; Dec 15, 2015; by Sanjay Zalavadia (VP of Client Service for Zephyr);

Agile software development provides major benefits to teams.

Agile can affect businesses differently based on the size and operational process integration methodology leveraged.

Agile's advantage can be offset by the journey to integrate it into 'your' organization.

A Dr. Dobb's Journal study identified that 68.6% of organizations were involved in an IT waterfall project that they knew would fail; however, agile projects were found to have a 71.5% success rate, compared with only 62.8% for other methods.
