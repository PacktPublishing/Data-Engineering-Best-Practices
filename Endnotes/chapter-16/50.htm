"What is Llama 2?"; by IBM;

Llama 2 is an entire family of transformer based autoregressive causal language models. These models are LLMs. Autoregressive language models take a sequence of words as input and predict output, or the next sequence of word(s). In Llama 2, base foundation models (FMs) are not pre-trained to answer a prompt, but rather text is appended in a grammatically correct way.

Refer to https://www.ibm.com/topics/llama-2.
