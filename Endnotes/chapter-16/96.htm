OneView; by OneView; 2024;

OneView accelerates ML training by using synthetic imagery to supercharge earth observation analytics.

The platform uses gaming engines to create 3D environments, delivering real-like satellite and airborne imagery.

OneView enables machine learning teams to speed up the training process.

See: "Synthetic Data: Has Its Time Come Yet?" (https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/synthetic-data-has-its-time-come-yet-tzahi-zack-weisfeld) by Tzahi (Zack) Weisfeld November 8, 2020.
