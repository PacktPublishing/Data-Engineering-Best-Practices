W&B Platform; 2024;

Weights & Biases (W&B) is an AI platform, with tools for training models, fine-tuning models, and leveraging foundation models. W&B Models and W&B Prompts are separate products from the W&B Platform that has capabilities across the ML lifecycle such as:

1.      Version and manage machine learning pipelines with auditable lineage to maintain the developed models and enable them to be reproducible.

2.      Explore and evaluate data and metrics via visualizations.

3.      Document and share insights with live reports which are clear and understandable by those who are non-technical.

4.      Query and create visualizations according to your needs.

Refer to https://docs.wandb.ai/guides/platform.
