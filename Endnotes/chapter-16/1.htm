"Thousands of AI Authors on the future of AI January 2024"; by Katja Grace, Harlan Stewart and Stephen Thomas (AI Impacts Berkeley, California); Julia Fabienne Sandkühler (Department of Psychology University of Bonn, Germany); Ben Weinstein-Raun (Independent); Jan Brauner (Department of Computer Science University of Oxford, UK); January 2024;

In this research study, which was funded by Open Philanthropy, located at 182 Howard Street #225. San Francisco, CA 94105, USA, and conducted in October 2023, the authors distributed a survey of perspectives about the future of AI to people who had recently published at one of six top-tier AI venues.

The survey was taken by 15% of those contacted with 2,778 actual researcher's responses considered.

Refer to https://arxiv.org/pdf/2401.02843.pdf.
