Modelbit; 2024;

Modelbit enables you to deploy ML from the tools you develop in like: Jupyter, Hex, Deepnote, Microsoft VS Code, etc.

A how to article by Moez Ali will help get you started with this product: "Easily deploy machine learning models from the comfort of your Notebook" (https://moez-62905.medium.com/easily-deploy-machine-learning-models-from-the-comfort-of-your-notebook-9068a88f4cf5).
