Neptune AI; 2024;

Neptune.ai features include:

1.     Compare experiments enables you to identify training strategies that perform better than others and the reasons why.

This supports optimized hypotheses driven experimentation iterations.

2.     Monitor model training maximizes real-time monitoring so that model divergence can be detected quickly.

3.     Reproduce experiments by tracking anything and reproducing everything via metadata that is used to reproduce the best results.

4.     Versions of models are supported in one place so that they can be tracked across your entire model lifecycle.

5.     Machine learning team collaboration capabilities allow your team to compare results, track progress, and communicate.

Refer to https://neptune.ai.
