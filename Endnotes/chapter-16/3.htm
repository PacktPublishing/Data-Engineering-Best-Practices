How worthy and hard is the alignment problem? Based on Stuart Russell, this question begins with a summary of Russell's argument.  Which claims that with advanced AI, you get exactly what you ask for, not what you want:

1.      Do you think this argument points at an important problem? 41% of the AI researchers said that the alignment problem is a very important problem.

3.      How hard do you think this problem is, compared to other problems in AI? A majority of AI researchers have said it is harder (36%) or much harder (21%) than other problems.
