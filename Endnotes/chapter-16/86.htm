DataGen (now bankrupt); 2024;

DataGen offered a self-service platform for synthetic data. The Datagen Platform used to give you granular control over data generation processes. This was done via neural network analysis so that the tool could generate the needed training data. High quality, variety, and domain specific simulated artificial data were outputs. Simulation of people and objects with context was possible (maybe dangerously or unethically so).
