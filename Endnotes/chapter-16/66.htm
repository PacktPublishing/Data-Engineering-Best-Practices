LlamaIndex.TS; 2024;

LlamaIndex works in two stages:

1.      indexing stage for preparing a knowledge base for use; and

2.      querying stage for getting relevant context from the knowledge base to assist the LLM in responding to questions.

This process implements retrieval augmented generation (RAG) capabilities of LlamaIndex.TS which provides a toolkit for making both steps easy.

Refer to https://ts.llamaindex.ai/getting_started/concepts.
