Context / categorical Variables; 2024; by Seldon;

The context aware maximum mean discrepancy drift detector as defined in "Context-Aware Drift Detection" (https://arxiv.org/pdf/2203.08644.pdf) by Oliver Cobb and Arnaud Van Looveren, 2022; is a kernel method for detecting drift by taking relevant context into account. Normal drift detectors detect distributions underlying two sets of samples. A context aware drift detector detects differences that can't be attributed to a corresponding difference between sets of associated context variables. It is used in Seldon's Alibi Detect product for drift detection.

Refer to https://docs.seldon.io/projects/alibi/en/latest/methods/CFProto.html#Categorical-Variables.
