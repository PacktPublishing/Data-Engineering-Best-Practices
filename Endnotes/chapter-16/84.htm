Betterdata; by Betterdata; 2024;

Betterdata allows you to generate and enhance synthetic data in four steps:

1.      Real User Data: begins with a dataset being made available;

2.      Training and Generation (has two sub-steps as follows):

a.      Synthetic Data Engine: generates representative synthetic data retaining the same insights as the original data using GenAI.

b.      Differentially Private Learning: provides privacy guarantees using Betterdata's Differential Privacy in order to supply enhanced protection of training records;

3.      Rigorous Quality Assurance: measures synthetic data quality using collected audit and compliance metrics;

4.      Limitless Data Enhancement: is provided to enhance data including imbalance correction, bias reduction, and intelligent filling of missing values.
