What is Amazon Kendra?; by Amazon; 2024;

With Amazon Kendra comes the capability to re-rank search results using:

Amazon Kendra Intelligent Ranking (https://docs.aws.amazon.com/kendra/latest/dg/intelligent-rerank.html).

It implements this feature by taking into account a search query's context, and all the available information from the search service documents.  The result is improved search results.

Refer to https://docs.aws.amazon.com/kendra/latest/dg/what-is-kendra.html.
