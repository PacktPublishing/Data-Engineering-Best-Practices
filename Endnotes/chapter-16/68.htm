Understanding Prompt Engineering Fundamentals; by Microsoft; 2024;

Prompt engineering can improve the specific performance of LLMs for some tasks. Contextual information helps guide the model's output. With engineered prompts, the LLMs are steered in the direction of specific relevant information that in turn leads to better results. The issue of catastrophic forgetting, (LLM forgets previously learned information) can take place when fine-tuning the model output for a given task. With designed prompts, the model can still retain relevant knowledge while adapting to new tasks.

Note: Prompt engineering is a fine-tuning approach for an LLM!
