Verta: "What is a Model Catalogue"; by Manasi Vartak; March 10, 2022;

 

"A model catalog is an inventory of all model assets in an organization that uses metadata to share knowledge assets created via data science, impose governance standards (including bias and fairness), streamline the usage of models across teams, and provide visibility into the performance and usage of models." - Source: Manasi Vartak
