F1-Score; by Wikipedia; 2024;

The F1-Score is a metric calculated from precision and recall to evenly optimize for both simultaneously.

The F1-Score is more precisely defined as the harmonic mean of precision and recall, and as such both are represented in one metric.

Note that relying on the F1-Score can be misleading since it supplies equal importance to both precision and recall.

An alternative is the P4-metric.

Refer to https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F-score.
