Cvedia; by Cvedia; 2024;

Cvedia offers synthetic data to solve the problem of collecting and labeling training data for computer vision models.

Synthetic data is generated using 3D models instead of collecting real data, similar to how Hollywood creates animations.

3D models describe the size, shape, and appearance of objects for a computer to visualize.

There is debate on whether it is always better than real data!

Synthetic data can be used to validate AI models.

It is a cost effective in regard to cost and scale of data collection efforts.
