Bias; 2024;

Bias in ML is a metric that is used to compare a model's predictions against expected outcomes. The mean squared error (MSE) (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mean_squared_error) is a measure of the average difference between a model's predictions and the true values. It is used to measure the percentage of correct predictions against the actual predictions. There are many types of bias like: algorithm, exclusion, measurement, prejudice, recall, selection, and sample to name a few.
