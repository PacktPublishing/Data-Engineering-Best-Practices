Fairlearn; 2024;

FairLearn is an open-source project to help improve the fairness of AI systems. The Fairlearn package consists of two components:

1.      Fairness Metrics: to assess groups negatively impacted by a model, and to compare models for fairness / accuracy metrics.

2.      Fairness Algorithms: to mitigate unfairness in AI models according to fairness definitions.

Refer to https://fairlearn.org.
