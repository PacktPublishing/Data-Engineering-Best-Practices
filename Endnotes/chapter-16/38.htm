"MLflow LLM Tracking"; 2024;

Mlflow's LLM Tracking system extends the MLflow Tracking system for monitoring, managing, and interpreting interactions with Large Language Models (LLMs). Enhanced are logging capabilities with distinct features tailored for LLMs like prompts which other model types exclude as a mechanism for preserving inference results. LLMs need this due to their generative nature. MLflow introduces the term predictions alongside the existing tracking components of artifacts, parameters, tags, and metrics, ensuring comprehensive lineage and quality tracking for text-generating models.

Refer to https://mlflow.org/docs/latest/llms/llm-tracking/index.html.
