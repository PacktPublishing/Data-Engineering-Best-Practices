OpenAI's ChatGPT 3.5; by OpenAI;

ChatGPT 3.5 isa fined tuned version of the GPT-3.5 algorithm and is a precursor to GPT-4.

It was launched in November 2022, and it is designed to be conversational and support natural language question and answering.

The approach used is to implement:

reinforcement learning with human feedback (RLHF) (https://aws.amazon.com/what-is/reinforcement-learning-from-human-feedback).

Refer to https://openai.com/gpt-4.
