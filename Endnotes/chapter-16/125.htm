Tensorflow Data Validation; 2024;

Tensorflow Data Validation analyzes training data and generate the following:

1.      Descriptive data statistics for data to be processed (https://github.com/tensorflow/metadata/blob/master/tensorflow_metadata/proto/v0/statistics.proto);

2.      Guess (or infer) a schema (https://github.com/tensorflow/metadata/blob/master/tensorflow_metadata/proto/v0/schema.proto) for the data; and

3.      Detect anomalies (https://github.com/tensorflow/metadata/blob/master/tensorflow_metadata/proto/v0/anomalies.proto) in the data.

Refer to https://www.tensorflow.org/tfx/data_validation/get_started.
