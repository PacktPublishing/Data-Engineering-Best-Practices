Servicenow - by ServiceNow; 2023. Supports a configuration management database (CMDB) application for building logical representations of assets, services, and relationships for enterprise infrastructure.

Component details are retained in the ServiceNow CMDB to:

1.      monitor,

2.      control,

3.      ensure integrity,

4.      provide stability, and

5.      continuous service operation of an infrastructure's configured items (CIs).

Refer to https://docs.servicenow.com/bundle/vancouver-servicenow-platform/page/administer/general/concept/capabilities-bundle-landingpage.html.
