NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5 "Security and Privacy Controls for Information Systems and Organizations" - by NIST.gov; September 2020 (includes updates as of Dec. 10, 2020).

The standard is a catalog of security and privacy controls for IT organizations to protect operations assets; individuals; other organizations; and the nation from threats and risks including: hostile attacks, human errors, natural disasters, structural failures, foreign intelligence entities and privacy.

Refer to https://nvlpubs.nist.gov/nistpubs/SpecialPublications/NIST.SP.800-53r5.pdf.
