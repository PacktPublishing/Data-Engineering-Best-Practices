A Data Factory is a collection of operational data flows producing a consumable data set which may be streamed or supplied in bulk manner toa consumer.

The data factory analogy works for organizations which seek to product data for consumption to end users.

This is often the case in most business products even if the consumer is an internal group.

But since data is the new oil it is not unreasonable to expect that data stand alone and be supplied to others in the absence of the business provided software delivery service.

The result is consumable data produced by the data factory.
