Microsoft Fabric Roadmap; October 5, 2023; announced its roadmap for the technology. In its overview, Microsoft identified that it offers a unified suite of analytical experiences that streamline data management.

Microsoft Fabric enables you to use data for competitive advantage today and prepare for future AI innovations.

Microsoft Fabric offers a data factory, data engineering, data warehousing, data science, real-time analytics, and business intelligence in a lake-centric SaaS platform.

Teams are able to process, analyze, and collaborate with data without having to focus on configuration or management of the underlying infrastructure.

Fabric's open foundation with its governance enables cloud and tool integration.

Maintaining data trust. Is a key capability.

It provides transparent cost control and optimizes expenses.
