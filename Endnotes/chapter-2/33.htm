Microsoft Azure Data Factory; 2023; enables you to integrate data with Azure Data Factory, a fully managed, serverless data integration service. You can visually integrate data sources with >90 built-in connectors.  ETL / ELT processes can be configured with or without code.  You can then deliver to consumers of your data: Azure Synapse Analytics to provide business insights. Refer to: https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/products/data-factory.
