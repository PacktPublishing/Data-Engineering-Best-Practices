AWS Data Pipeline; 2023; is a web service to process and move data between different AWS compute and storage services, as well as on-premises data sources, at specified intervals. You can access your data where its stored, transform, process it, and transfer results to AWS services such as:

1.    Amazon S3,

2.    Amazon RDS,

3.    Amazon DynamoDB, and

4.    Amazon EMR.

Refer to: https://aws.amazon.com/datapipeline.
