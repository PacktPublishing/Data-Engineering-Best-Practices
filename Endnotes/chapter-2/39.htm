Pervasive Software; 2023; is a company that was acquired by Actian Corporation in 2013, that provided: Pervasive Data Integrator and Pervasive Data Profiler integration products, and the Pervasive PSQL relational database management system (formerly: Actian Zen, and before that Btrieve, was renamed Pervasive PSQL).  Goldstar Software carries the product line today: Pervasive Software Products. Refer to: https://communities.actian.com.
