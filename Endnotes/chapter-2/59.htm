Stack Overflow; 2023; is a question-and-answer website for programmers. It is the flagship of the Stack Exchange Network and was created in 2008 by:

1.    Jeff Atwood (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeff_Atwood) and

2.    Joel Spolsky (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joel_Spolsky).

Questions and answers on certain computer programming topics are supported as an open alternative to earlier Q&A websites such as Experts-Exchange. Stack Overflow was sold to Prosus, a Netherlands-based consumer internet conglomerate, on 2 June 2021 for $1.8 billion. Refer to: https://stackoverflow.com.
