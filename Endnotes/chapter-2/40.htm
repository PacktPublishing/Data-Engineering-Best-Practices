Polytomic; 2023; Polytomic is a low-code/ no-code platform for piping data to Salesforce, Marketo, HubSpot, ZenDesk, or other system.

Its sync engine automatically syncs incrementally (CDC) and uses the right bulk APIs, rate limits, and throttling in its smart job queuing logic.

Its features are: universal data model; no coding; real-time syncing; minimal API calls; auto monitoring, logging, and recovery; detailed error notifications; >40 integrations; first-class SQL and spreadsheet support; per-field sync modes; auto cross-system joins; sync summary reports; and large-scale data support.

Polytomic Connect allows you to sync to and from a customer 's system; pull from SaaS tools, databases, data warehouses, and REST APIs into your own data warehouses, databases, business applications, and spreadsheets; or reverse ETL sync from your data warehouses, SaaS tools, databases, and APIs to data warehouses, business applications, spreadsheets, APIS, and cloud storage.
