OrientDB; 2023; is a multi-model open source NoSQL DBMS that brings together graphs and flexibility of documents into a scalable high-performance operational database.

It was engineered with performance in mind.

It's fast for both read and write operations.

It stores up to 120,000 records per second.

It is a powerful and flexible operational DBMS.

Though OrientDB Community Edition is free for commercial use, enterprise applications need additional functionality.

OrientDB Enterprise Edition provides features of our community edition plus:

1.      incremental backups;

2.      security;

3.      24x7 support;

4.      query profiler;

5.      distributed clustering configuration;

6.      metrics Recording; and

7.      live monitor and configurable alerts.

Refer to: https://orientdb.org.
