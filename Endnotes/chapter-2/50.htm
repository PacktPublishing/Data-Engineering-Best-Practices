Stitch; 2023;  is a cloud-first, open source platform for rapidly moving data. It is an ETL service. It connects to data source with >140 source connectors.  Databases like MySQL and MongoDB, as well as SaaS applications like: Salesforce and Zendesk  are integrated. Capabilities include:

1.    a data analysis service (via analytics partners who can assist);

2.    a data visualization / querying tool (since Stitch only moves data); and

3.    a destination (aka a data warehouse of your choice).

Refer to: https://www.stitchdata.com.
