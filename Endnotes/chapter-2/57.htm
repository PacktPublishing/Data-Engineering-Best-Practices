Workato; 2023; is one platform for intelligent integration & workflow automation across your organization. You can integrate data, and automate processes, publish APIs, build robust data integrations, create custom apps and bots, and work with event streams. The Common Workato Runtime (CWR) provides scaling that lowers operational costs and minimizes downtime. Template based cookbooks are sharable to others.
