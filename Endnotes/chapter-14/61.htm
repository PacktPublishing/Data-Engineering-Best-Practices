Throtle; 2024;

Throttle provides data clean rooms (DCRs) that protect healthcare data and personal identifiable information (PII).

You are given control over what and how information gets shared.

Throtle supports collaboration within existing client data clean room environments. The Throtle clean room is independent from your cloud or data center and connects to your data source (AWS or Snowflake). No data transformation or unnecessary data movement is required.

Advanced encryption and differential privacy technologies ensure strict controls and permissions.

Refer to https://www.throtle.io/clean-room-and-collaboration.
