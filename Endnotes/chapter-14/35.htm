Signal Messenger, LLC; 2023; Developed the "Signal Messenger App." (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Signal_(messaging_app)) as an encrypted messaging service for instant messaging, voice, and video calls.

Instant messaging includes: text, voice notes, images, videos, and other media.

Group messaging is also supported. Signal uses mobile telephone numbers as individual user identifiers.

The Signal Foundation has a vision:

"Protecting free expression and enabling secure global communication through open source privacy technology." - Source: Signal’s Mission (https://signalfoundation.org).

 

Refer to https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Signal_Foundation.
