Claravine Data Clean Rooms; 2024;

With the end of the third-party cookie support, and Apple's IDFA (https://www.branch.io/resources/blog/what-is-the-idfa-learn-what-its-used-for-and-why-ios-14-killed-it) deprecation; agencies, brands, publishers, and walled gardens need to find alternative measurement and attribution strategies.

The Claravine Data Standards Cloud helps organizations clarify and apply data standards at scale.

"A 2021 Claravine study revealed that 63% of advertisers are expecting privacy changes to negatively impact their global view of campaign performance. Clean rooms offer a safe harbor to share privacy-sensitive data while adhering to data privacy compliance laws, such as GDPR and CCPA." - Source Claravine; 2021.
