Zeotap in May 2022, Zeotap and Decentriq (as DCR SaaS provider), announced their partnership to unify CDP and DCR capabilities.

By assigning a unique identifier to each customer, cross channel entity matching is facilitated.

The CDP unifies your first-party data; rendering it usable in a DCR.

The DCR generates value from matched first-party data in the absence of having third-party cookies available for analytics.

This is all done in a GDPR-compliant way.
