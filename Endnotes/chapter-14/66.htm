AWS Budgets; 2024;

AWS Budgets allows you to take action on your AWS costs / usage. Monitoring cost is the goal.

Optimizing Reserved Instances (RIs) or Savings Plans, is possible.

See: Best practices for AWS Budgets For more information (https://docs.aws.amazon.com/cost-management/latest/userguide/budgets-best-practices.html).

Refer to https://docs.aws.amazon.com/cost-management/latest/userguide/budgets-managing-costs.html.
