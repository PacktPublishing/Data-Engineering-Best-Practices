Edgeless Systems; 2024;

Edgeless Systems is a leading confidential computing platform.

They offer an open source solution that works across clouds. The Constellation technology is 100% Kubernetes from the inside, and 100% shielded from the outside. MarbleRun orchestrates secure enclaves and confidential containers. And Ego enables you to build apps for secure enclaves in Go. Three levels of confidential computing are supported:

1.      Level 1: Protect keys;

2.      Level 2: Protect single containers;

3.      Level 3: Protect all deployments from end-to-end, without gaps.

The success story from a privacy-preserving data exchange for the financial industry states:

"We can now build a safe lock with confidential computing between companies so that data sets can be exchanged in a secure and privacy preserving way. This was not possible before because of competition and privacy issues." - Giuseppe Giordano, R&D Senior Manager at Accenture Intro to confidential computing in finance (https://www.edgeless.systems/resource-library/confidential-computing-in-finance-webinar-with-accenture).
