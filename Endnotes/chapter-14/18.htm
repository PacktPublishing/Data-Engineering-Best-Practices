". . . effectively managing cash flow is crucial for the success and sustainability of any organization. Cash flow management involves monitoring, analyzing, and optimizing the movement of cash into and out of a business. One powerful tool that can help businesses achieve these goals is TIBCO Spotfire, a leading data analytics platform." - Source Zebra.ai; 2023-08-20 ("How to Improve Cash Flow Using TIBCO Spotfire" (https://zebrabi.com/guide/how-to-improve-cash-flow-using-tibco-spotfire);

"Hemlock Semiconductor uses Spotfire manufacturing analytics to optimize outputs and lower costs. $300K savings per month in electricity consumption via analytic-driven asset utilization.
