AWS: What Is an In-Memory Database?; by AWS; 2024;

In-memory databases are used when applications require microsecond response times or have uneven traffic loads (like gaming, session stores, and real-time analytics).

The terms main memory database (MMDB), in-memory databases (IMDS), and real-time databases (RTDBs) are types of in-memory databases.
