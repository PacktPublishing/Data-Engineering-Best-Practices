Epsilon Data; 2024;

Epsilon PeopleCloud is a product built on Epsilon Customer that unifies your data with insights into customer behavior outside your brand. It doesn't rely on first-party data or require third-party cookies to stay connected to your customers.

The unifying ID is the Identity Core ID (https://www.epsilon.com/us/products-and-services/identity-core-id).

Refer to https://www.epsilon.com/us/products-and-services/data and https://www.epsilon.com/us/products-and-services/epsilon-peoplecloud/customer-data-platform.
