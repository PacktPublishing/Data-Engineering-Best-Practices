"How do Data Clean Rooms (DCR) work?"; 2023;

In this article a DCR is enabled for two or more parties that collect, compile, and aggregate first-party-data sets at the user level. These data sets match on some defined criteria.

Matching can take place in the DCR based on matching criteria (i.e., email addresses, phone numbers, or user IDs).

Loading into the secured and encrypted (also hashed, pseudonymized, access restricted & obfuscated) environment enables the acre to be quarantined off as a data clean room. This is possible if the supplied data adheres to all of the predetermined data contracts between the parties (first-party-data owners, stewards, and consumers).
