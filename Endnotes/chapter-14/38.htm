Habu; 2024;

Habu, now owned by LiveRamp, was founded in 2018. Habu is a DCR technology platform.  Data does not have to move into Habu for Habu to implement its DCR technology.

A primary investor was Snowflake. Habu partners well with cloud providers and big data systems like: AWS, GCP, Azure, Databricks and Snowflake. Read this research paper:

IDC MarketScape - Worldwide Data Clean Room Technology for Advertising and Marketing Use Cases 2023-2024 Vendor Assessment; by Lynne Schneider (https://www.idc.com/getdoc.jsp?containerId=US51607923)
