Netskope; 2024;

Netskope is a security service edge (SSE) tool used to secure access to the web, cloud services and private applications.

Critical capabilities include:

1.      access control;

2.      threat protection;

3.      data security;

4.      security monitoring; and

5.      acceptable use control.

These are enforced by network-based and API-based integrations.

SSE capabilities are primarily delivered as cloud-based services with or without agent-based components.

ML is used in Netskope to improve the quality of sensitive content being moved in the enterprise.

It also has a catalog of SaaS applications being monitored with risk scores for connected applications. Eight SIEM integrations exist with Netskope.

Refer to https://www.netskope.com.
