Kaspersky Security for Internet Gateway; 2024;

The Kaspersky Security for Internet Gateway offers risk reduction in areas including: infection, data leaks and internet distractions.

Capabilities include:

1.      multi-layered gateway-level protection against web-based threats;

2.      blocking infections (before they can reach endpoints);

3.      negating social engineering effects;

4.      complementing existing gateway defenses;

5.      reducing attack exposure by controlling internet resource usage;

6.      supporting regulatory compliance initiatives;

7.      allowing management of multiple independent workspaces; and

8.      helping to detect attacks and providing automated responses.
