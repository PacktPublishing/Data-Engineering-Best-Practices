Sprinto; 2024;

Sprinto is a single platform to manage compliance needs. Tools and support are provided to organize, monitor, and scale security compliance.

Many out-of-the-box compliance programs are supported with automated compliance workflows and continuous monitoring.

Audit frameworks supported:

1.      Primary audit frameworks:

a.      SOC 2: monitors five SOC 2 trust services criteria;

b.      ISO 27001: manages ISO 27001 certification and surveillance audits;

c.      GDPR: maintains compliance with EU data privacy law;

d.      HIPAA: creates / monitor healthcare compliance programs; and

e.      PCI-DSS: centrally maps PCI-DSS controls and continuously monitors compliance.

2.      Additional audit frameworks:

a.      ISO 27017: ISO/IEC 27017 information security framework for organizations using cloud services. It keeps cloud service customers safe by providing comprehensive approaches to security;

b.      FCRA: The Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) regulates consumer reporting and auditing the collection of this type of information is supported;

c.      CIS: Critical Security Controls (CIS) auditing is supported;

d.      OFDSS: Open Finance Data Security Standard (OFDSS) auditing is supported;

e.      NIST: NIST Cybersecurity Framework is also supported;

f.        CCPA: The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) auditing is supported; and

g.       CSA Star: CSA STAR Certification process is supported.
