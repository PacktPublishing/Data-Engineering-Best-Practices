"Simplifying data mesh for Self-Service Analytics on an Open data lakehouse"; by Mike Ferguson, Intelligent Business Strategies, Dremio; 2023;

In data mesh designs, decentralized teams of domain-oriented subject matter experts (SMEs), build data pipelines for data products, that result in datasets being curated for others use.

The domain owner owns the data product.

Self-service analytics is conducted on the data infrastructure software provided. Federated data governance is enabled.

Data privacy policies must be applied to sensitive data in various data products while data owners retain access control for their data products.
