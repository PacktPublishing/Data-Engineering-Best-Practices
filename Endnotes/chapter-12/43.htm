"D&B Data Exchange"; 2020;

D&B Data Exchange is a way for third parties to share data on the D&B platform.

It is called: DEX and contains ~12 partners of information (as of 2020) on the D&B system:

1.      Aberdeen;

2.      DataProvider;

3.      EDA (Rand Reilly);

4.      IQVIA;

5.      Iridium;

6.      Netwise;

7.      Oxxford;

8.      POLK (IHS Markit);

9.      Profound;

10.  US Farm Data;

11.  Wealth Engine; and

12.  TNS (Kantar)

Refer to https://www.dnb.com/content/dam/english/dnb-data-insight/DNB_Data_Exchange_Partner_Deep_Dive.pdf.
