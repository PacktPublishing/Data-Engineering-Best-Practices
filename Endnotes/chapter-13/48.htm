IBM (RedBook) Foundational Ontologies for Smarter Industries; by Dr. Akram Bou-Ghannam, IBM Executive IT Architect with the IBM Software Group Industry Solutions (https://www.linkedin.com/in/akram-boughannam); 2013. This IBM Redpaper describes foundational concepts for implementing smarter industry solutions to optimize industrial processes, operations, and improve decision making (analytics).
