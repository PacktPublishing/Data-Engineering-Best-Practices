Data Pipelines & Data Mesh: Where We Are and What the Future Looks Like; QCon Plus (https://qconferences.com); May 17-28, 2021. A conference session facilitated by Fabiane Nardon (https://plus-archive.qconferences.com/plus2022/hosts/fabiane-nardon) to discuss the current challenges for building modern data pipelines and applying data mesh in the real world, what the future looks like, and tools. Zhamak Dehghani, Tareq Abedrabbo and Jacek Laskowski participating:

1.       Zhamak Dehghani (https://www.linkedin.com/in/zhamak-dehghani) was Director of Emerging Technologies at Thoughtworks,  the creator of the Data Mesh concept and now the CEO and founder of Nextdata (https://www.linkedin.com/company/nextdata-technologies).

2.       Tareq Abedrabbo (https://www.linkedin.com/in/tareqabedrabbo) was Core Data Principal Engineer at CMCMarkets (https://www.linkedin.com/company/cmc-markets), and now the Data CTO at Mesh-AI (https://www.linkedin.com/company/wearemesh-ai).

3.       Jacek Laskowski (https://www.linkedin.com/in/jaceklaskowski) is an IT freelancer, java champion and author of The Internals Of online books (https://github.com/japila-books).
