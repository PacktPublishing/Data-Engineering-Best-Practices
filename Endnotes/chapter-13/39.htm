Dataiku - How-To: Execute Anomaly Detection at Scale in 5 Easy Steps; October 18, 2019; by Nancy Koleva (https://medium.com/@nancy.koleva/about), Dataiku.

“Anomaly detection is an approach that can be useful across an array of industries and for a variety of purposes. But the underlying, unifying factor is the ability to detect small changes or differences in a system that might otherwise go unnoticed. Uncovering anomalies using machine learning allows humans (or other systems) to take action based on these outliers. The ultimate goal of anomaly detection is the value of the insight that outliers provide. That is, for a business, money saved from preventing equipment damage, money lost on fraudulent transactions, etc … Anomaly detection has a large variety of real-world applications, but how do you get started? … Understand the basics and learn the 5 key steps to implementing anomaly detection at scale ...” - Nancy Koleva

1.      understand the business;

2.      get as much data with as much variety as you can;

3.      explore, clean, and enrich your data;

4.      visualize (data that can be an outlier); and

5.      go to production with live data sets to make real time predictions.
