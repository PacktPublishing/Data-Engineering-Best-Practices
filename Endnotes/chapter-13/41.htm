Databricks Unity Catalogue; 2023; is a unified governance solution for data and AI on the Databricks Lakehouse. See this brief for a definition of the tool: Data, Analytics and AI Governance How to build an effective governance strategy for your data lakehouse (https://www.databricks.com/resources/ebook/data-analytics-and-ai-governance)   With the Unity Catalog, structured and unstructured data may be governed. Machine learning models, notebooks, dashboards, and files can be registered. Data scientists, analysts and engineers use the catalog to discover, access and collaborate on data assets. It is key to the lakehouse architectures effectiveness. A unified governance of data and its metadata facilitates AI development efforts. Refer to: https://www.databricks.com/product/unity-catalog.
