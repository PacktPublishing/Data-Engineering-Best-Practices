Market Data; 2023; Wikipedia; A Financial Ticker Plant is a market data consolidator of real time market information with historical data and time series processing capabilities. A commercially available financial ticker plant example is : Exegys Ticker Plant (https://www.exegy.com/products/exegy-ticker-plant); and a non-commercially available ticker plant example is the London Stock Exchanges Group Ticker Plant (https://www.londonstockexchange.com/equities-trading/market-data/group-ticker-plant). Refer to: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Market_data.
