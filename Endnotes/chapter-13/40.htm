Why Semantics Matter in the Modern Data Stack; 2023; AtScale; by: Dave Mariani (https://www.linkedin.com/in/davidpmariani) founder and Chief Technology Officer of AtScale. David is a Big Data visionary and serial entrepreneur. He founded AtScale after experiencing the challenges of scaling enterprise analytics and AI/ML to cloud scale at Yahoo and Klout. AtScale defines a semantic layer (https://www.atscale.com/glossary/semantic-layer) as being:

“the business representation of data which offers a unified and consolidated view of an organizations data. In a semantic layer data definitions from different data sources are mapped to product a unified, consistent, view of information for analytics purposes.”

The benefits of a semantic layer are:

1.      Democratization of data analytics and machine learning (ML)

2.      Single source of truth

3.      Seamless model development and sharing

4.      Improved query performance and reduced computing costs

5.      Reduced data cleaning effort

6.      Better security and governance

Refer to: https://www.atscale.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/White-Paper-Modern-Data-Stack.pdf.
