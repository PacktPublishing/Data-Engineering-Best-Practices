Schema.org; 2023; Schema.org is an OWL definition with limited reasoning capability. Any translation to OWL means adding axioms to Schema.org to increase the inferences that can be drawn from Schema.org documents. Schema.org provides a current OWL file here: Schema.org Current RDF (https://schema.org/version/latest/schemaorg-current-https.rdf) with the additional axioms to enable richer reasoning.  See: Schema.org Datamodel (https://schema.org/docs/datamodel.html) and refer to: https://schema.org.
