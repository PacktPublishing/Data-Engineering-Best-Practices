Collaborative platform tools that facilitate Agile methodologies range from JIRA (https://www.atlassian.com/software/jira), a robust platform for project management and issue tracking, to Confluence (https://www.atlassian.com/software/confluence), which serves as a collaborative workspace for team documentation.

Asana (https://asana.com) is another web and mobile application designed for team organization and workload management.

Trello (https://trello.com) offers a visual approach to project management with its board and card system.

Basecamp (https://basecamp.com) provides real-time communication capabilities, ideal for straightforward projects and task coordination.
