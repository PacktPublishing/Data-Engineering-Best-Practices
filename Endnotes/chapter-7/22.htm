"ITIL V4 (EasyVista ITILV4 in a Nutshell)"; 2021;  ITIL V4 was released in 2019.

This eBook covers:

1.      What changed with ITIL 4;

2.      The key components of ITIL 4; and

3.      The benefits of ITIL when employed with ITSM tools.

'34 ITIL V4 Management Practices' are identified which are divided into three categories:

1.      General management practices;

2.      Service management practices; and

3.      Technical management practices.

Modern ITSM tools are designed ITIL V4 best practices and they apply automation to improve IT operations.

Enterprise service management or digital transformation strategies are accelerated.

The ITSM tool selected delivers benefits of ITSM and ITIL V4 and provides a platform for digital transformation with benefits like:

1.      better, faster, and cheaper operations / outcomes;

2.      omnichannel service and support capabilities;

3.      knowledge capture to improve operational outcomes;

4.      operational performance insights through dashboards / reporting; and

5.      rapid improvement, agile responsiveness and innovation.
