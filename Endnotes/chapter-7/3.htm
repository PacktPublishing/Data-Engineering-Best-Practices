AWS Workload Discovery; 2023; by Amazon AWS;  formerly called AWS Perspective is a tool used to visualize AWS cloud workloads.

Use Workload Discovery on an AWS account to build, customize, and share detailed architecture diagrams of workloads based on live data from AWS.

Diagrams may be shared other AWS users or outside of Workload Discovery via export to PNG, JSON, CSV, or draw.io.
