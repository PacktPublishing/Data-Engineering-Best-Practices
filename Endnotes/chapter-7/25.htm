Open Groups Open Agile Architecture; 2023;

The Open Groups Open Agile Architecture is a standard of The Open Group, and is designed with consideration for the needs of various businesses stakeholders:

1.      Business Leaders driving an enterprises digital and agile change processes;

2.      Enterprise Architects scope of influence in an agile development enterprise;

3.      Product Managers transforming customer experiences & producing innovative products while generating growth;

4.      Product Owners accelerating transformation & developing valuable product features for  delivery;

5.      Operations Managers  enabling lean, automated sustainable competitive advantage; and

6.      Software Engineers leveraging digital technology to collaboratively innovate with the business.

Refer to https://www.opengroup.org/AgileArchitecture
