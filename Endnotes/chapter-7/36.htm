ITOps vs. DevOps: whats the difference?; August 8, 2023;

DevOps means development and operations.

ITOps is the traditional practice or an organization servicing information technology and operations.

ITOps exercises a culture of caution.

DevOps primary focus is to iteratively develop new applications.

DevOps engineers bridge gaps between development and information technology, reducing silos and workflow redundancy.

There is overlap between DevOps and ITOps, but they have different purposes within an organization.
