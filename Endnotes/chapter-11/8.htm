Devops Maturity Assessment; SourceGroup; 2023.

The SourceGroup's assessment is for leaders of technology, development or operations who are responsible for an organization's engineering processes, operational compliance, and innovative enablement.

The assessment's focus is on people, processes, and tooling.

It compares current practices to matured DevOps teams and organizations.

Refer to https://www.sourcedgroup.com/devops-maturity-assessment/.
