"Observability Primer"; 2023; is a primer defining key terms like:

1.      Observability;

2.      Telemetry;

3.      Reliability;

4.      Metrics;

5.      Service level indicator (SLI);

6.      Service level objective (SLO);

7.      "Spans" find "span-context" on this page: (https://opentelemetry.io/docs/concepts/signals/traces);

8.      Structured log messages;

9.      Other metadata (aka attributes);

10.  Distributed trace;

11.  Baggage (https://opentelemetry.io/docs/concepts/signals/baggage); and

12.  Semantic conventions (https://opentelemetry.io/docs/specs/semconv).

Refer to https://opentelemetry.io/docs/concepts/observability-primer.
