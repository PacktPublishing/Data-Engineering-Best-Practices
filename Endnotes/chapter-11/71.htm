DORA Metrics; 2023;

DORA Metrics (Explained) see: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jYQSH4EY6_U) includes four key metrics in two core DevOps areas as follows:

1.      Team Velocity:

a.      Deployment frequency, and

b.      Lead time;

2.      Stability:

a.      Change failure rate, and

b.      Time to restore service.

Refer to https://octopus.com/devops/metrics/dora-metrics.
