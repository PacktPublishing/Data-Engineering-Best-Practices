"The 3 pillars of observability": Logs, metrics, and traces; June 7, 2022; by Chris Tozzi (TechTarget);

These three pillars of observability provide three perspectives for a system's operations.

Taken as a whole and analyzed to determine what's happening within a complex environment; insights are evidenced regarding a solution's operational characteristics. Teams should not limit themselves to these three pillar data sources alone such as metrics and data from the CI/CD pipeline may be used to add color to what is operating and therefore providing inputs aligned to the three pillars.

Additionally, business data for segmented audiences can help pinpoint erroneous business rules or logic that arise ONLY in niche areas.

Cloud service outputs provide for systems context in which your solution is running and that can be used to isolate non-functional faults in the solutions design or operational characteristics.
