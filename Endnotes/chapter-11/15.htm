BITOL; 2023;

BITOL is an Open Source Linux Foundation AI & Data Sandbox Project (under the Apache 2.0 license - https://lfaidata.foundation) that is a fork from the data contract work from Jean-Georges Perrin jgp (jgperrin - https://github.com/jgperrin) located in https://github.com/jgperrin/data-contract-template.

BITOLs mission is stated to develop a "set of standards to define data contracts, data products; to tackle multiple data engineering challenges, such as data normalization, ensuring the relevance of documentation, establishing service-level expectations, simplifying data and tool integration, and promoting a data product-oriented approach.".

The "Open Data Contract Standard (ODCS)" (https://www.linkedin.com/company/bitol-io), has and is being developed in support the and advancement of the concepts of the Data Mesh architecture.

Refer to https://github.com/bitol-io.
