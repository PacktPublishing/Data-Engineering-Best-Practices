"Build a modern, distributed Data Mesh with Google Cloud"; by Firat Tekiner (Product Management, Google) and Susan Pierce, Senior Product Manager, Google; October 20, 2022;

First discussed in: "Build a Modern, Distributed Data Mesh with Google Cloud" (https://services.google.com/fh/files/misc/build-a-modern-distributed-datamesh-with-google-cloud-whitepaper.pdf) February 2022.

An example is given that contains three data domains:

1.    Sales;

2.    Supply chain and

3.    Products.

Each domain hosts a number of products, each owned / maintained by their business teams.

These teams leverage a Google Cloud Platform (GCP) for self-service data supported by a core team defining identity access management (IAM), modeling, security, compliance, and architecture best practices.

Refer to: https://cloud.google.com/blog/products/data-analytics/building-a-data-mesh-on-google-cloud-using-bigquery-and-dataplex.
