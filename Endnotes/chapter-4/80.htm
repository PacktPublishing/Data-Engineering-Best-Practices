'Grock'; 1961; Grock is a slang term coined by Robert A. Heinlein (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_A._Heinlein) in 1961 (author and aeronautical engineer ) in "Stranger in a Strange Land" (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stranger_in_a_Strange_Land)

It is defined as meaning, "to understand intuitively or by empathy, to establish rapport with and to empathize or communicate sympathetically (with); also, to experience enjoyment" (https://www.oed.com/search/dictionary/?q=grok&tl=true).

Refer to: https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/grok.
