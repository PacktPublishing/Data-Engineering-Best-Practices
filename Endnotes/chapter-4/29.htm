Knowledge Base; 2023; a Knowledge Base is a set of sentences in a knowledge representation language (refer to the meaning of 'Knowledge Graph') , with interfaces to provide answers about what is known or inferred by data relationships.

A knowledge base is a technology solution that stores semantically structured data.

The initial use supported the development of expert systems, or the first knowledge-based systems.
