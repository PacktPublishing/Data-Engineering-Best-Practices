Rockset Concepts, Design & Architecture; by Purvi Desai & Kevin Leong, Rockset; June 2022;

Rockset is a real-time analytics database for building scalable data-intensive applications.

Application developers are enabled to create fast APIs leveraging SQL search, aggregation and join queries on semi-structured data.

The architecture of Rockset and its capabilities reflect advanced design choices and innovations that enable efficient complex distributed queries across various data sets.
