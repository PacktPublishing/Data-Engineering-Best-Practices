"Metrics to Evaluate your Machine Learning Algorithm"; by Aditya Mishra; Feb 24, 2018;

1.    Classification Accuracy;

2.    Logarithmic Loss;

3.    Confusion Matrix;

4.    Area under Curve;

5.    F1 Score;

6.    Mean Absolute Error; and

7.    Mean Squared Error.

Also as pointed out by Tavish Srivastava On August 6, 2019 (last updated on July 22, 2023) in "12 Important Model Evaluation Metrics for Machine Learning Everyone Should Know (Updated 2023)" (https://www.analyticsvidhya.com/blog/2019/08/11-important-model-evaluation-error-metrics/) are:

1.    Gain Chart and Lift Chart;

2.    Kolomogorov Smirnov Chart;

3.    Gini Coefficient;

4.    Concordant Ratio and Discordant Ratio;

5.    Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE);

6.    Root Mean Squared Logarithmic Error;

7.    R-Squared / Adjusted R-Squared; and

8.    Cross Validation.
