Data catalogs are solutions that inventory and provide information about data. These catalogs are a new type of data silo with issues like:

1.    a rigid data representation model;

2.    limited in meaning associated with data sets;

3.    lacking a semantic search capability;

4.    missing concept connectivity (i.e., semantic mappings); and

5.    implemented as closed, proprietary systems.

Also, TopBraid EDG 7.7 (https://archive.topquadrant.com/doc/7.7) is a knowledge graph creation and curation tool, with open, non-restrictive ways to inspect, validate, improve quality, and add value to data than other data catalogs.

TopQuadrant's metadata management solutions make it possible for organizations to:

1.    understand data, Connect data silos and trace data lineage;

2.    increase productivity through understanding impacts of proposed data changes, enable collaboration;

3.    improve business decisions by create digital twins to combine, integrate, exchange, and analyze information more effectively and reliably;

4.    reduce business risks by knowing how, and when shared data can be used.
