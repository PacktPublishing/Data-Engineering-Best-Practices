Semantic Web; 2023; Sometimes known as Web 3.0 (not Web3), is a World Wide Web (WWW) extension by the WWW Consortium (W3C). The Semantic Web goal is to make internet data machine-readable.

In May 17, 2001 in a Scientific American article: "The Semantic Web"  by Tim Berners-Lee, James Hendler & Ora Lassila (https://pdfs.semanticscholar.org/566c/1c6bd366b4c9e07fc37eb372771690d5ba31.pdf),  described the expected evolution of the existing WWW to a 'Semantic Web'
