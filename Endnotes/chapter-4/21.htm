Tim Berners-Lee; Sir Tim Berners-Lee (OM, KBE, FRS, FREng, and FRSA) invented the World Wide Web in March 1989; Tim is co-founder and CTO of Inrupt.com (https://www.inrupt.com), that develops the open-source ‘Solid’ platform (https://solidproject.org).

The Solid Protocol uses W3C standards to define a user-centric method of storing and sharing data.

He is author of: "Weaving the Web: The Original Design and Ultimate Destiny of the World Wide Web;" (https://www.amazon.com/Weaving-Web-Original-Ultimate-Destiny/dp/006251587X) December 16, 2022 ; ASIN: B0BQFCH3TR.
