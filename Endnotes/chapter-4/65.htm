InfluxDB Comparisons; 2023; InfluxDB Comparisons is a github repo containing code for benchmarking InfluxDB against other databases and time series solutions.

Three examples are the reports:

1.    InfluxDB vs Elasticsearch (https://get.influxdata.com/rs/972-GDU-533/images/InfluxDB_vs_Elasticsearch.pdf),

2.    InfluxDB vs Cassandra (https://get.influxdata.com/rs/972-GDU-533/images/InfluxDB_vs_Cassandra.pdf), or

3.    InfluxDB vs Graphite (https://get.influxdata.com/rs/972-GDU-533/images/InfluxDB_vs_Graphite.pdf).

Refer to: https://github.com/influxdata/influxdb-comparisons.
