Graphite; 2023; Graphite is an open-source software (FOSS) tool which monitors time-series data such as the performance of computer systems.

It was developed by Orbitz Worldwide, Inc (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orbitz) and was released as open source in 2008.

Graphite collects, stores, and displays time-series data in real time. No 'Graphite TSBS Benchmark' exists for it, but InfluxDB has produced a benchmark to its offering: InfluxDB Comparisons

Refer to: https://github.com/graphite-project/graphite-web.
