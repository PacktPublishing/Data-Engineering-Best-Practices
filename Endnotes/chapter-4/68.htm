Databricks Lakehouse and Data Mesh, Part 1 (and Part 2); by Sharon Richardson, Bernhard Walter, Pawarit Laosunthara, Guillermo Schiava D'Albano, Fran Medina Castro and Amr Ali; October 10 and 19, 2022; in "Platform Blog" (https://www.databricks.com/blog/category/platform);  

Databricks Lakehouse capabilities support the Data Mesh pattern from an architectural point of view.

Data Mesh and Databricks Lakehouse patterns arose to address common pain points and shortcomings of data warehouses and data lakes.

The data mesh pattern comprehensively articulates the business vision.

Databricks Lakehouse provides a foundation to meet those needs particularly:

1.    'Unity Catalog' (https://www.databricks.com/product/unity-catalog) as an enabler for independent data publishing, central data discovery, and federated computational governance; and

2.    'Delta Sharing' (https://delta.io/sharing) via Databricks, (https://docs.databricks.com/en/data-sharing/index.html) to efficiently and securely share fresh data between business domains across organizational boundaries.

Refer to: https://www.databricks.com/blog/2022/10/10/databricks-lakehouse-and-data-mesh-part-1.html, and https://www.databricks.com/blog/2022/10/19/building-data-mesh-based-databricks-lakehouse-part-2.html.
