Toc2; 2024;

The Toc2 Jupyter extension enables you to collect a notebook's running headers and display them in:

1.      A floating window;

2.      As a sidebar; or

3.      With a navigation menu.

The Toc2 extension also features automatic page numbering with unique links ids, and an optional table of contents cell.

The sections of selected or running cells are highlighted in the table of contents.

The table of contents can be saved as exported html.

Refer to https://jupyter-contrib-nbextensions.readthedocs.io/en/latest/nbextensions/toc2/README.html.
