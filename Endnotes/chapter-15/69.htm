SQLBI; 2024;

SQLBI is a Microsoft Gold Partner consulting firm that produces a number of training books for Microsoft Press, on SQL and business intelligence with a focus on Microsoft technologies especially:

1.      AAS (https://www.sqlbi.com/training/analysis-services);

2.      DAX (https://www.sqlbi.com/training/dax);

3.      Data Modeling (https://www.sqlbi.com/training/data-modeling); and

4.      Data Visualization (https://www.sqlbi.com/training/data-visualization).

Examples are:

1.      DAX Patterns - Second Edition (https://www.sqlbi.com/books/dax-patterns-second-edition);

2.      The Definitive Guide to DAX - Second Edition (https://www.sqlbi.com/books/the-definitive-guide-to-dax-2nd-edition); and

3.      Analyzing Data with Power BI and Power Pivot for Excel (https://www.sqlbi.com/books/analyzing-data-with-microsoft-power-bi-and-power-pivot-for-excel).

Refer to https://www.sqlbi.com/about.
