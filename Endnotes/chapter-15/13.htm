CoCalc; 2024;

CoCalc is a virtual online workspace for calculations, research, collaboration, and authoring documents. CoCalc consists of Projects, and these projects partition your work into separate workspaces.

Project consist of files permissioned to you and your collaborators.

Files conforming to the naming convention *.sagews are for working with SageMath (as in Sage Worksheets), and an *.ipynb file starts a Jupyter notebook.

Likewise, a *.tex file opens an editor for LaTeX documents.

Refer to https://cocalc.com/features/jupyter-notebook.
