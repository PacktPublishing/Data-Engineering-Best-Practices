Commuter; 2024;

Commuter is an nteract (https://nteract.io) focused server. It reads notebooks from a local directory or Amazon S3, has a directory explorer to find notebooks, and provide a Jupyter compatible version of the contents via an API.

You can determine where your notebooks reside and where they are to be shared.

Refer to https://github.com/nteract/commuter.
