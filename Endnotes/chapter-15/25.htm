Paperspace by Digital Ocean; February 26, 2024;

Paperspace is a cloud-based machine learning platform with GPU powered virtual machines and a Kubernetes based container service.

Paperspace offers notebooks, deployments, workflows, and machines for developing, training, and deploying AI applications.

Notebooks are comprised of a web based Jupyter solution with persistent storage for long-term shared development collaboration with accelerated compute capabilities.

Refer to https://docs.digitalocean.com/products/paperspace.
