Explorium; 2024;

Explorium is a data platform tool for :

1.      predictive prospect mining,

2.      lead scoring / qualification,

3.      customer relationship management (CRM) / account enrichment,

4.      total available market (TAM) / strategic analysis, and

5.      supply chain analysis.

A key technical focus is on data quality as this statement points out:

"When it comes to data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence, the consensus is that good data is essential. When it comes to training models, you commonly hear "garbage in, garbage out". This means that a model will only be as good as the data you feed it with, therefore, data quality is important." - Source: Noam Cohen, Data Products Team Leader (data science expert), Explorium (https://www.explorium.ai/blog/the-data-people/data-quality-an-interview-with-a-data-science-expert).
