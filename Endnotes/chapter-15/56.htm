NBDime; 2024;

NBdime provides a way of diffing and merging Jupyter Notebooks.

Tool features are:

1.      nbdiff compares notebooks;

2.      nbmerge is a three-way merge of notebooks with conflict resolution;

3.      nbdiff-web is a rich rendering of diff'd notebooks;

4.      nbmerge-web is nbmerge for the web;

5.      nbshow presents a notebook in terminal-friendly format.

Refer to https://github.com/jupyter/nbdime.
