DAX Patterns is a collection of DAX patterns.  See (https://www.daxpatterns.com/patterns), taken from the book DAX Patterns, Second Edition; by Marco Russo and Alberto Ferrari (SQLBI); ISBN-10: 1735365203; ISBN-13: 978-1735365206.

2.      Basket analysis;

3.      Budget;

4.      Comparing different time periods;

5.      Cumulative total;

6.      Currency conversion;

7.      Custom time-related calculations;

8.      Dynamic segmentation;

9.      Events in progress;

10.  Hierarchies;

11.  Like-for-like comparison;

12.  Month-related calculations;

13.  New and returning customers;

14.  Parameter table;

15.  Parent-child hierarchies;

16.  Ranking;

17.  Related distinct count;

18.  Semi-additive calculations;

19.  Standard time-related calculations;

20.  Static segmentation;

21.  Survey;

22.  Time patterns;

23.  Transition matrix; and

24.  Week-related calculations.
