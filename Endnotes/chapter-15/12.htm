Google Colab; 2024;

Aliased as Google Colaboratory this is a free cloud Jupyter Notebook environment.

Programming languages supported include: Python, R, and Julia. Being on GCP gives the notebooks access to GCP resources, including GPUs and TPUs, to run machine and deep learning models.

Colab Enterprise (aka Google Vertex AI Notebooks (https://cloud.google.com/vertex-ai-notebooks?hl=en) combines Google Researchs Notebooks with Google Clouds enterprise security; and for those working on the GCP cloud platform, the advantages are compelling.

Refer to https://colab.research.google.com.
