"Atlan - Integrate Data Pipeline Metadata with Assets"; 2024;

Atlan produces a cloud-based data democratization platform to help manage data ecosystems.

Capabilities exist for:

1.      data discovery;

2.      data catalog;

3.      active data governance; and

4.      embedded collaboration.

Regarding data lineage, you can:

5.      Add data pipeline custom metadata (https://ask.atlan.com/hc/en-us/articles/6755053022225-What-is-custom-metadata) in Atlan; 

6.      Begin by defining the attributes you desire from your data pipelines into your asset profiles; 

7.      Highlight your most critical metadata with badges (https://ask.atlan.com/hc/en-us/articles/8603249978781-How-to-add-custom-metadata-badges) to help users obtain the asset data contexts; and

8.      Follow the Atlan best practices (https://ask.atlan.com/hc/en-us/categories/8291012145937-Community) guide to utilize the data lineage metadata to implement your x-ray like functionality across you data factories pipelines.

Refer to https://ask.atlan.com/hc/en-us/articles/8292932755857-Integrate-data-pipeline-metadata-with-assets.
