Calculation Groups; by SQLBI; 2024;

In Microsoft's Analytics Architecture Calculation Groups apply calculations into DAX measures.

Calculation groups are used to implement time intelligence calculations (e.g., year-to-date, year-over-year, and currency conversion etc).

They also enable the selection of a measure through a slicer.

Best practices are identified in this article to avoid modeling design issues.

Refer to https://www.sqlbi.com/calculation-groups.
