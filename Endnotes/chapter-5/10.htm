Smart Data is digital information formatted in a way that it can be acted upon before being sent to downstream processing stages and eventually analytics consumers.

It is smart in that it is self-contained  (to preserve context) or has formal interfaces denoted already to existing information; thus, leading to consolidation into domain knowledge and therefore useful analytics.

Eventually, through data pipeline processing stages, you can envision how smart data leads to new insight discovery through the highly connected nature of the systems knowledge.
