C4ISR; 1997; C4ISR stands for Command, Control, Communications, Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance (C4ISR) Architecture Framework, Version 2.0.

It was developed by the U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) and provides guidance for describing architectures, intended to ensure architectures developed by:

1.      Commands;

2.      Services; and

3.      defense agencies

The above are inter-relatable between operational, systems, and technical architecture views, and are comparable and integrate-able across joint multi-national organizational boundaries.
