Precisely Data360; 2023; is a solution with integrated capabilities to streamline the data supply chain from information collection and discovery, through insights and outcomes.

It gives you the power to trust your decisions based on quality data. Data governance provides feature to let you know where data comes from, what it means, who owns it and whether it is trusted.

It delivers 'basic measures' such as: 'completeness,' 'conformance' and 'validity' and advanced capabilities around: 'reconciliation,' 'streaming data,' and 'enhanced scorecards/dashboards.'
