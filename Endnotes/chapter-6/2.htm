Medallion Architecture; 2023; Databricks;

'What is a medallion architecture?' Answer:

1.       It is a data design pattern.

2.       It is used to organize data (logically) in a lakehouse.

3.       Its goal is to incrementally and progressively improve the structure / quality of data as it flows through each layer of the architecture (from Bronze ⇒ Silver ⇒ Gold).
