TigerGraph; 2023; delivers a scalable graph database and analytics platform for technical and non-technical users.

It's an enterprise scale graph designed for speed and analytics.

It is one of the first systems capable of real-time analytics on web-scale data.

The 'native parallel graph (NPG)' design has a focus on storage and computation, supporting real-time graph updates and built-in parallel computation.

The SQL-like 'graph query language (GSQL)' provides ad-hoc exploration and interactive analysis of big data.

With GSQL's capabilities and NPG speed, you will be able to perform 'Deep Link Analytics' that uncovers connections in and between data, that were previously too impractical to reach or cumbersome to explicitly define.
