Oracle Enterprise Data Quality; 2023; provides a comprehensive data quality management environment, used to understand, improve, protect, and govern data quality.

It is geared to facilitate best practice systems (as well as other applications and cloud services):

1.       'master data management (MDM),'

2.       'data governance,'

3.       'data integration,'

4.       'business intelligence,'

5.       'data migration,' and

6.       'integrated data quality in customer relationship management (CRM)'.
