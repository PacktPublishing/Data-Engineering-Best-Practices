ManageEngine Applications Manager; 2023;  is an all-in-one monitoring solution for IT administrators, DevOps and 'Site Reliability Engineer (SRE)s'.

Visibility into the performance of complex enterprise applications and cloud workloads is provided.

You can identify potential issues, improve productivity, and ensure that users and data consumers are not affected by downtimes or poor performance.

Features supported are:

1.       '>150 enterprise apps supported;'

2.       'application performance monitoring (APM);'

3.       'end-user experience monitoring;'

4.       'cloud and container monitoring;'

5.       'AI-assisted smart alerts;' and

6.       'AI powered analytics.'
