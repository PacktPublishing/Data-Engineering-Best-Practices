Azure Monitor; 2023; allows you to collect, analyze, and act on telemetry data from cloud and hybrid environments.

Azure Monitor supports operations at scale by maximizing performance and availability of your resources and proactively identifying problems.

Features include:

1.       'curated insights for a customized monitoring experience;'

2.       'Visualization tools to observe ingested data;'

3.       'a powerful data platform for deeper troubleshooting, diagnosis & analysis;' and

4.       'critical situation response handling of near-real-time alerts with the ability to auto scale resources when load increases.'

Refer to: https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/products/monitor
